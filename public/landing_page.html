About ADS

Australian Dietary Supplements (ADS) helps you make informed choices about vitamins, supplements, and over-the-counter products. ADS brings key product info into one place so you can save time and money.

Our price comparison fetches data from leading Australian retailers. Browse products, check details and ratings, and quickly spot value. You can also save favourites and track prices over time.

Need quick guidance? ADS presents simple, helpful information to support smarter decisions. (Always remember: this is not a substitute for professional medical advice.) With a clean interface and mobile-friendly design, ADS aims to be a trustworthy companion for health product research.
